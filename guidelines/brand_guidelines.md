# Brand Guidelines
This document represents brand guidelines for Yoga Lab & Lab Coffee.

## Brand Pillars
Yoga Lab and Lab Coffee offers the transformationabl power of yoga both on and off the mat. Through classes, coffee, and community, they together create an experience for each and every person to access the foundational tools of regulation, relationship, and reasoning to empower them to live rich lives pursuing the work they love.

Pillars:
- **Regulate**: In the yoga studio, you will experience a deeper level of physical and mental health through regulated movement and breathwork.
- **Relate**: In the coffee shop, you will meet new people and experience a sense of community wellness.
- **Reason**: In the lab, you will have the opportunity to share your knowledge and expertise to support reasoning and critical thinking in our community.

## Brand Colors
This section represents the brand colors. Please note that these colors were created with the whole business in mind, including color for real life stuff like furniture and such to place in the studio / coffeeshop.

Primary colors:
- **Black**: Hex: #000000. Best for text and emblems on white backgrounds. Also makes a great dark background.
- **Light Gray**: Hex #e5e6ea. Best for text and emblems on black backgrounds.
- **White**: Hex #ffffff

Secondary colors:
- **Dark Gray**: Hex #4f4e4e. Best for accent decor around the studio space and merch items.
- **Tan**: Hex #d9cdba. Best for accent decor around the studio space to bring in some warmth to the color palette.

## Fonts

### Font Families
Gotham:
- **About**: Gotham is a sans serif font that is iconic, bold, clean, and modern. It is a well-loved typeface with a large family of variations to choose from.
- **Usage**: Gotham is the primary typeface for the brand. It should be used for headers, subheaders, and body text on all materials.

Cormorant Garamond Italic:
- **About**: Cormorant Garamond is an open-source serif font family. Its serifs make it appear elegant and trustworthy. It was chosen to contrast the stark modern lines of Gotham to provide a clean and beautiful accent typeface.
- **Usage**: Cormorant Garamond is to be used very rarely. When used, it is only an accent typeface and can call out specific questions in a large body of text or special headings.

### Font Usage

- **Heading**: Your header font will be used for large messaging and headings. It's purpose is to clearly communicate the main message. Use Gotham Bold in all-caps. Add 100em of spacing between letters.
- **Subheading**: Your subhead font will only be used when small text that is indicating a category of importance is needed. It's purpose is for small titles and to communicate secondary messaging. Use Gotham Medium in all-caps. Add 100em of spacing between letters.
- **Body Copy**: Your paragraph font will be used as body copy. It is meant to be easy to read when large amounts of copy is needed. Use Gotham Book in sentence case.
- **Accent**: Your accent font will be used minimally to display secodnary messaging that you wish to draw special attention to. Use Cormorant Garamond Italic in title case.
